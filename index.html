A note from the proprietor

Welcome, friend.
Hang your coat.

Whichway Escape is not, strictly speaking, a game. It is an evening — the kind one tells stories about for years afterward. Our chambers are designed in the manner of a fine library: every detail chosen, every clue set with intent.

Whether you arrive in a party of two or a confederacy of eight, you will find no flashing lights nor frantic timers shouting at you. Only a low fire, a curious puzzle, and the quiet pride of having figured the thing out yourselves.

— The Proprietor

The Dossier

How an evening unfolds.

  1. i.

    Reserve your chamber

    Choose a room and a time. We seat one party at a time — you'll never share a chamber with strangers.

  2. ii.

    Arrive at the parlour

    A short brief by the fire. A drink, if you like. Then the doors are closed behind you.

  3. iii.

    Solve the matter

    Sixty minutes on the clock. No jump-scares, no timer alarms. Just three puzzles and your wits.

  4. iv.

    Take your portrait

    Win or lose, every party leaves with their photograph in our book. The best names go on the wall.
